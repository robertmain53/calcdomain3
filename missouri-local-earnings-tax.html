The Formula Explained

Tax Amount = Income × (Tax Rate / 100)

Glossary of Terms

  • Income: The total gross earnings before any deductions.
  • Tax Rate: The percentage at which income is taxed locally.
  • Tax Amount: The total amount of tax to be paid.

Example: A Step-by-Step Guide

For a person earning $50,000 annually with a local tax rate of 1%, the tax amount will be calculated as follows:

Tax Amount = $50,000 × (1 / 100) = $500
