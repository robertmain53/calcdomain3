Formula

The conversion from pounds to kilograms uses the following formula:

\[ \text{kg} = \text{lbs} \times 0.45359237 \]

This conversion factor is exact and based on the international avoirdupois pound definition.

Data Source and Methodology

Glossary of Terms

Pound (lb or lbs)
A unit of weight/mass commonly used in the United States and other countries. Equal to exactly 0.45359237 kilograms.
Kilogram (kg)
The base unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI). Used globally as the standard unit of mass.
Avoirdupois Pound
The standard pound used for most purposes, as opposed to the troy pound used for precious metals. This converter uses the avoirdupois pound.
