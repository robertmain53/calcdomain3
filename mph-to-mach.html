MPH to Mach Converter

Convert speed in miles per hour (mph) to Mach number (multiple of the speed of sound at sea level, std. atm.). Essential for aircraft, rockets, and science applications.

Formula

Mach = mph ÷ 761.207

Standard: 1 Mach = 761.207 mph at sea level, ISA (15°C, 101.325 kPa). To convert, divide the mph value by 761.207.

Example Calculation

  • 1522.41 mph ÷ 761.207 = 2.00 Mach
  • Result: 1522.41 mph = Mach 2

FAQ

How do you convert mph to mach?

Divide the speed in mph by 761.207. Example: 500 mph ÷ 761.207 = 0.66 Mach.

When is this calculator useful?

Whenever you want to relate speeds in mph to multiples of the speed of sound (jets, rockets, high-speed wind tunnels, physics).
