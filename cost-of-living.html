The Formula Explained

The cost of living index is calculated as follows:

Equivalent Salary = (New City Index / Current City Index) * Current Salary

Glossary of Terms

  • Current City: The city you currently live in.
  • New City: The city you are considering moving to.
  • Salary: Your current annual salary.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

Consider moving from New York City to San Francisco. If your current salary is $100,000, the calculator helps determine what salary you would need in San Francisco to maintain the same standard of living.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the cost of living?

The cost of living refers to the amount of money needed to cover basic expenses such as housing, food, taxes, and healthcare in a particular place and time.

How often is the data updated?

The data is updated annually or as new data becomes available.
