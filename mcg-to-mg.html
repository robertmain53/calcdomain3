The Formula Explained

The formula for converting micrograms to milligrams is: \( \text{mg} = \frac{\text{mcg}}{1000} \)

Glossary of Variables

  • mcg: Micrograms
  • mg: Milligrams

Practical Example

Example: Converting 5000 mcg to mg. Using the formula, \( \text{mg} = \frac{5000}{1000} = 5 \, \text{mg} \).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a microgram?

A microgram (mcg) is a unit of mass equal to one millionth of a gram.

What is a milligram?

A milligram (mg) is a unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a gram.

Why is this conversion important?

This conversion is crucial in fields such as pharmacology and nutrition where precise measurements are required.
