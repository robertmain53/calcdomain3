The Formula Explained

The sum of a sequence is calculated by adding all numbers in the sequence. The average is the sum divided by the count of numbers.

Glossary of Terms

  • Sequence: An ordered list of numbers.
  • Series: The sum of the elements of a sequence.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a series and sequence?

A sequence is an ordered list of numbers, and a series is the sum of a sequence of numbers.

How do you determine if a series converges?

A series converges if the sequence of its partial sums tends to a limit; otherwise, it diverges.
