The Formula Explained

The formula used in this calculator is:

P = √3 × V × I × cos φ

Glossary of Variables

  • Voltage (V): The potential difference between two points.
  • Current (A): The flow of electric charge.
  • Power Factor (cos φ): The ratio of real power to apparent power.
  • Power (kW): The rate at which energy is transferred.

FAQ

What is three-phase power?

Three-phase power is a method of alternating current electric power generation, transmission, and distribution.

How do you calculate three-phase power?

Three-phase power can be calculated using the formula: P = √3 × V × I × cos φ.
