The Formula Explained

\( \text{sq m} = \frac{\text{sq ft}}{10.7639} \)

Where sq ft is the area in square feet and sq m is the area in square meters.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the standard conversion rate: 1 square meter = 10.7639 square feet, or equivalently, 1 square foot = 0.092903 square meters. This is the internationally recognized conversion factor for area measurements.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 1000 square feet to square meters:

  1. Start with the formula: \( \text{sq m} = \frac{\text{sq ft}}{10.7639} \)
  2. Substitute 1000 for sq ft: \( \text{sq m} = \frac{1000}{10.7639} \)
  3. Calculate: \( \text{sq m} = 92.903 \)

Result: 1000 sq ft = 92.90 sq m

Glossary of Terms

  • Square Feet (sq ft or ft²): An imperial/US customary unit of area measurement. One square foot is the area of a square with sides of one foot.
  • Square Meters (sq m or m²): A metric unit of area measurement. One square meter is the area of a square with sides of one meter.
  • Conversion Factor: The fixed number (0.092903) used to convert square feet to square meters, or equivalently divide by 10.7639.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why convert square feet to square meters?

Square meters are the international standard for area measurement used in most countries. Converting from square feet is essential when dealing with international property transactions, construction projects, or scientific applications.

How many square meters are in one square foot?

One square foot equals approximately 0.092903 square meters. This means a square foot is much smaller than a square meter.

Is this tool accurate?

Yes, this calculator uses the standard conversion factor (dividing by 10.7639) and displays results to two decimal places, providing accuracy suitable for real estate, construction, and everyday applications.

Can I convert square meters back to square feet?

Yes! To convert square meters back to square feet, multiply the square meter value by 10.7639. For example: 100 sq m × 10.7639 = 1076.39 sq ft.

What's a typical apartment size in square meters?

A typical 1000 square foot apartment equals approximately 92.9 square meters. A spacious 2000 square foot home is about 185.8 square meters.
