The Formula Explained

Taxes Due = Conversion Amount × (Marginal Tax Rate / 100)

Glossary of Terms

  • IRA Balance: The total amount currently in your IRA.
  • Marginal Tax Rate (%): The tax rate you pay on an additional dollar of income.
  • Conversion Amount: The amount you plan to convert from a traditional IRA to a Roth IRA.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a Roth IRA conversion?

A Roth IRA conversion involves transferring funds from a traditional IRA into a Roth IRA, which may result in taxable income.

Why consider a Roth conversion?

Roth conversions can be advantageous for tax-free withdrawals in retirement and for estate planning.
