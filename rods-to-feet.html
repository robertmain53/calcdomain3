All calculations are based on the conversion factor provided by historical data sources. Specifically, 1 rod is equal to 16.5 feet. Source: UnitConverters.net. All calculations are based strictly on this data.

The Formula Explained

The conversion formula from rods to feet is simple:

Feet = Rods × 16.5

Glossary of Terms

  • Rod: A unit of length, equal to 16.5 feet.
  • Feet: A unit of length in the imperial system, equal to 12 inches.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, if you have 10 rods, the conversion to feet would be:

10 rods × 16.5 = 165 feet
