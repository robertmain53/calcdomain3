The Formula Explained

The IRR is calculated using the formula:

NPV = ∑ (Cash Flow / (1 + IRR)^n) - Initial Investment = 0

Glossary of Terms

  • Initial Investment: The initial amount of money invested.
  • Cash Flows: The money generated by the investment each year.
  • IRR: The rate at which the net present value of cash flows equals zero.
