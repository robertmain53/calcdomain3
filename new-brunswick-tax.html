The tax rate is determined by the provincial government based on income brackets.

Can I use this calculator for other provinces?

No, this calculator is specifically for New Brunswick. Rates differ in other provinces.

Are the results guaranteed to be accurate?

This calculator provides estimates based on current tax rates. For precise calculations, consult a tax professional.
