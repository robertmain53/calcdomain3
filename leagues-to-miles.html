Glossary of Terms

  • League: An ancient unit of length approximately equal to 3 miles or 4.8 kilometers.
  • Mile: A unit of length equal to 5280 feet or approximately 1609 meters.
  • Kilometer: A unit of length in the metric system equal to 1000 meters.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a league?

A league is an ancient unit of length that was commonly used in Europe. It is approximately equal to 3 miles or 4.8 kilometers.

How do I convert leagues to miles?

To convert leagues to miles, multiply the number of leagues by 3.

How do I convert leagues to kilometers?

To convert leagues to kilometers, multiply the number of leagues by 4.82803.

Where were leagues commonly used?

Leagues were commonly used in Europe and parts of the Americas.

Are leagues still used today?

Leagues are not commonly used today and are considered an outdated unit of measurement.
