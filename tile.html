The Formula Explained

Grout Formula: Grout = (Tile Area × 1.5 kg/m²)

Mortar Formula: Mortar = (Tile Area × 3 kg/m²)

Glossary of Terms

  • Tile Width: The width of a single tile in centimeters.
  • Tile Height: The height of a single tile in centimeters.
  • Total Area: The total area to be tiled in square meters.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate is this calculator?

The calculator provides estimates based on standard material requirements; actual needs may vary.

Can I use this for floor and wall tiles?

Yes, the calculator is suitable for both floor and wall tiling projects.

What if my tiles are not square?

For non-square tiles, use the longest side as the width and the shortest as the height for approximation.

How should I prepare the surface?

Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and level before applying grout and mortar.

Is this calculator suitable for outdoor projects?

Yes, it can be used for both indoor and outdoor tiling projects.
