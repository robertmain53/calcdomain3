Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of barcodes can I generate?

Our tool supports a range of barcode types including UPC, EAN, and Code 128.

Is there a limit to the data I can input?

The amount of data you can input depends on the barcode type selected. Please refer to specific barcode type limitations.

Can I use the generated barcode commercially?

Yes, you can use the barcodes generated by this tool for commercial purposes.

How can I ensure my barcode is scannable?

Ensure that the barcode is printed at a standard size and that the contrast between the bars and the background is high.

Does this tool support QR codes?

Yes, QR code generation is supported and can be used for encoding URLs, text, and other data.
