Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the data provided by The Oracle of Bacon. All results are derived from their extensive database and algorithms.

The Formula Explained

The Bacon Number algorithm calculates the shortest path between two actors in a collaboration graph. The graph is constructed from movie credits, and the shortest path is found using a breadth-first search algorithm.

Glossary of Terms

  • Bacon Number: The number of degrees of separation an actor has from Kevin Bacon.
  • Actor: The person whose connection to Kevin Bacon is being calculated.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

To calculate Tom Hanks' Bacon Number, we check the movies he's in and find the shortest path to a movie featuring Kevin Bacon. The path length gives the Bacon Number.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon?

It is a game based on the concept of six degrees of separation, which suggests that any two people on Earth are six or fewer acquaintance links apart.

How do I use this calculator?

Simply enter the name of an actor and click calculate to find out their Bacon Number.

Can I use this tool for any actor?

Yes, as long as the actor's filmography is included in the database from The Oracle of Bacon.

Is Kevin Bacon really connected to every actor?

Not every actor, but the concept is a fun way to explore the interconnectedness of the film industry.

What if I can't find an actor?

Ensure the spelling is correct and try again. If the issue persists, the actor might not be in the database.
