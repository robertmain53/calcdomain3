The Formula Explained

Glossary of Terms

  • Pascals (Pa): The SI unit of pressure.
  • PSI: Pounds per Square Inch, a unit of pressure.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why convert Pascals to PSI?

PSI is commonly used in the United States, while Pascals are used internationally. Converting between them is necessary for various engineering projects.

What is the conversion factor from Pascals to PSI?

The conversion factor is 0.0001450377.

How do I use this converter?

Enter the value in Pascals and click the 'Convert' button to get the PSI equivalent.

Is this tool accurate?

Can I convert PSI back to Pascals?
