Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a slug?

A slug is a unit of mass used in the US customary system, primarily in physics.

How do I convert slugs to kilograms?

Use the formula: Mass in kg = Mass in slugs × 14.5939

Why use kilograms instead of slugs?

Kilograms are the standard unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI), making them more universally recognized.
