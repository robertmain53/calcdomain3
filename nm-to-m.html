The Formula Explained

Meters = Nanometers ÷ 1,000,000,000

Glossary of Terms

  • Nanometer: A unit of length equal to one billionth of a meter.
  • Meter: The base unit of length in the International System of Units (SI).

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 1,000,000 nanometers to meters, divide 1,000,000 by 1,000,000,000 to get 0.001 meters.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a nanometer?

A nanometer is a unit of length in the metric system, equal to one billionth of a meter (0.000000001 m).

How do I convert nanometers to meters?

To convert nanometers to meters, divide the number of nanometers by 1,000,000,000.
