Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a corticosteroid conversion calculator?

It is a tool used to convert dosages between different corticosteroids based on their relative potencies.

Why is steroid conversion important?

Conversion ensures that patients receive an equivalent therapeutic dose when switching medications.

How accurate is the conversion?

The conversion is based on established medical guidelines, but clinical judgment should be applied.

What are common steroid types?

Common steroids include prednisone, dexamethasone, and hydrocortisone.

Can this tool be used for all steroids?

This tool is designed for commonly used corticosteroids. Consult medical guidelines for others.
