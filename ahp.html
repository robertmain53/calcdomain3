Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is AHP?

AHP stands for Analytic Hierarchy Process, a structured technique for organizing and analyzing complex decisions.

How accurate is the AHP method?

The AHP method is highly accurate when criteria and alternatives are well-defined and comparisons are consistent.
