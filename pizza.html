The Formula Explained

Pizzas Needed = (Number of People × Slices Per Person) ÷ Slices Per Pizza

Glossary of Terms

  • Number of People: The total number of people attending.
  • Slices Per Person: How many slices each person will eat.
  • Pizzas Needed: The total number of pizzas you should order.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How many slices are in a standard pizza?

A standard pizza typically has 8 slices.

Can I adjust for different pizza sizes?

Yes, you can adjust the calculation based on the number of slices per pizza.
